SURF COACHING


This package is optimal for those that have not tried surfing before, Those with who have basic surfing skills, or those of an intermediate level looking to further enhance their ability. Our experienced surf instructors will bring you to the best surf spots according to your surfing skills and the surf conditions. Prime wave and wind conditions will let you make huge advances in learning to surf and will improve you to the next level of surfing. We will cater the instruction based on your needs with our small group coaching. You will learn theories and etiquette of surfing, and arm yourself with the skills to learn to surf on your own. Starting to surf the whitewater and going onto catch the green waves we will be with you every step of the way..



What can I expect?

Beach safety and surf equipment

Positioning yourself correctly on the surfboard and in the ocean

How to paddle, pop up and ride your first wave in the broken white water waves

How to get ‘out back’ to the green unbroken waves and ride them to the beach

How to play with speed and turn in both directions

You will learn about rips, tides and surfers’ etiquette
