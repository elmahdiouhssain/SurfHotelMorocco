SURF

 

The Surf Hotel, one of the leading surf camps in Morocco, offers the best in surf lessons for beginners, surf guiding for experienced surfers, which you can combine with daily yoga to help keep your body in balance. Located in the hills of the Tamraght Ou Fella, on the north African coast of Morocco, we are surrounded by world class surf breaks. Half way between Agadir and Imsouane, nestled between the villages of Aourir and Taghazout, the hotel is a 10-minute walk to the closest beach in front of the newly developed Taghazout Bay complex. The region is famous for its 330 days of sun per year, with the summer months bringing consistent swell conditions perfect for beginners, and the winter swell serving up the big waves Morocco is renowned for. The list of surf spots surrounding the hotel is endless, all accessible with our surf coaching and surf guiding packages, including breaks like Anza, Panorama, Taghazout, Anchor Point, Mysteries, Killer Point plus so many more along the coast to Imsouane. With views of the closest breaks “CroCro” and “Devils Rock” from our beautiful rooftop terrace, you can check out the waves in the morning before our coaches, using their local insight and surf experience, take you to find the best spot for the daily surf conditions to create the most optimal experience for your surf trip.
